🌐HTML

🎨CSS

I’ve spent time learning CSS to bring design into my projects. With it, I can control colors, fonts, and layouts, making websites more visually appealing and user-friendly. I’ve practiced spacing, margins, and alignment to keep things clean and organized. Beyond the basics, I’ve experimented with flexbox and responsive design, making sure pages look good on different screen sizes. CSS is what transforms a plain HTML page into something that feels alive and polished.”

Even though I’m not there yet, it’s the next step on my learning path.
